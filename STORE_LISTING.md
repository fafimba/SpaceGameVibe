## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Pilot your ship through endless waves of enemies in this fast-paced space survivors game.

Your weapons fire automatically — you focus on dodging, positioning, and making split-second decisions. Every run is different: choose from 11 unique weapons across 30+ upgrade paths, craft powerful builds, and push deeper into the swarm.

GAMEPLAY
- Auto-fire combat: your ship shoots, you survive
- Physics-based movement with precise controls
- Hordes of enemies that grow in numbers and difficulty
- Quick sessions: 3–8 minutes per run

PROGRESSION
- 11 weapons, each with 10 upgrade levels
- Weapon evolutions at max level
- Permanent upgrades between runs
- Crystal economy to unlock new weapons

FEATURES
- Neon geometric visual style
- Touch controls with virtual joystick
- Runs smoothly on older devices
- No ads. No pay-to-win. Just gameplay.

Can you survive the swarm?
